# Friday, April 10th, 2026

### 22:05 UTC-04

Once again, I had the opportunity to watch history being made live! After around
10 days in space, and going behind the moon and back, the Artemis II mission has
finally splashed down, safely, back to Earth. Being able to witness this accomplishment
live brings me the same feeling that I have leaving the cinema after a science-fiction
movie: it is a weird sensation of "unrealness" - that what you just watched is not
real life, but it is! According to the plans that have been publicized, humanity
should be back to The Moon in 2027 or 2028! I'm really looking forward to it.
